    About The Project

    Big Blue Sea is an online fishmonger based in Grimsby UK, They work with local fishermen to produce a high quality of sustainable fish in the UK and supply one of the UK biggest supermarket.The design takes inspiration from the sea and fishing itself, focusing on elements such as fish hooks, wave, and sails to inspire the letterforms. For example, the “L” in the center of the design has a distinctive sharp that reflect fish hooks with many of the tails refecting that too. This paired with the flowing descenders over the other letterforms reflecting waves give the logo a nautical and modern feel.
