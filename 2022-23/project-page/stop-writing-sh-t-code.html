    Skills Used: Illustrator and Indesign

    About The Project

    When working full time for a technology company I was asked to create a new book cover by one of the developer managers working at the company. His main role was to allocate developers to products and ensure any problems were communicated to and from clients. part of his role was also working with QA (Quality Assurance) and BA (Business Analyst) to find these problems and taking them to people who can solve them. The book was based on his experience looking over other code and finding these problems and simple mistakes that can easily be fixed.The design is based on some of the emojis the team would send to each other on messaging service which gives the cover a playful feel with such an abrupt title to it. This with the clean look of the cover helps to give it a professional look before seeing the poo emoji.
