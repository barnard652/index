    About The Project

    Over the past year, I have used the Apple Watch Fitness app to track my swimming and keep a log of all my past swims. I am now getting to a stage where the app isn't giving me the information I need to improve my swim, with no form of feedback in the swim session. This project aims to give the user a higher level of feedback simply and clearly that improves the user swimming.
