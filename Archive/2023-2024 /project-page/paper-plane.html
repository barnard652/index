    About The Project

    Paper Plane is a flight logging app designed for groups of people who love flying, from small groups of friends who need a way to keep track of their aircraft flight records and documents, to clubs and organizations such as flight schools and gliding clubs. Using a clean and simple design, this system is designed to keep logging aircraft simple and easy.

    Activly changing status are simple and easy to understand with clean animation and text.
