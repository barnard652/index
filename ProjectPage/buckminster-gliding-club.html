Project:
Buckminster Gliding Club Visual Identaty & Branding

Skills used; Photoshop, Illustrator, Typography, Logo Design, Branding, Packaging Concepts.

About the Project

Buckminster Gliding Club has had the same look for many years now, even with a new member, new gliders and new direction the club graphics look and feel have stayed the same. However, this has all started to changes with the start of 2019. The visual styling and representation via media platforms didn't seem to represent important aspects of the club. Ferfer more the old visual style of the club is not comparable in its different forms (print, web, digital, etc). Using a new methodology that drives the club forward in this decade, this rebrand will help to form a new club mentality and promote existing aspects to members and to the public.

The New logo and visual style of the club have started a new set of changes to the club and the way it presents itself to the public and the wider gliding community as a whole.
