PROJECT:
SCEEN PRINTED POSTER FOR DEGREE SHOW.

Skills used; Photoshop, Poster Design, Screen Print.

About The Project

The branding of the 2017 Graphic Communication Degree Show is centred around the idea of speed and how fast our time at university has gone. The name ‘Zero To 100’ reflects this and the poster design brings this into the printed form. Using two scenes to layer and create a moving effect carries this theme over to the poster as well as giving the design a handmade look, making each truly individual.
With the help of members of the class and a day in the print room we where able to create 65 of these posters to use at the end of year show. Credit; Jerry Brito (holding the poster) design and printing.
