Project:
2017 Degree Show Poster Design and Screen Printing

Skills used; Photoshop, Poster Design, Screen Print.

About the Project

The branding of the 2017 Graphic Communication Degree Show at The University of Northampton was centred around the idea of speed and how fast our time at university has gone. The name ‘Zero To 100’ reflects this and the poster design brings this into the printed form. Using two scenes to layer and create a moving effect carries this theme over to the poster as well as giving the design a handmade look, making each truly individual.
