PROJECT:
JUST MAKE STUFF, FONT & LETTER DESIGN.

Skills used; Illustrator, Photoshop, Typography, Productive Procrastination.

About The Project

Productive procrastination is a term I give to work that is created when I personly need to stop doing a current project and focus my energy on other outlets for a short time. This can be out of design but sometimes working on these project can lead to something that fits well in my portfolio and illustrates my try feeling, in this case, it is my passion to simply make and create new things. ‘Just Make Stuff’ is a personal motto I have carried with me throughout the university, my professional and my personal life, it has pushed me to start new projects both in and out of graphics, focusing on what is possible in the field of creative work and what is possible for me personally. The overall graphics style of this design is meant to be rough like many of our frost ideas, something that appears to be constructed and handmade in a simple yet elegant fashion.

Other Projects; Error Message Board, flow branding, Buckminster Gliding Club
