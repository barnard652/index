Project:
Paper Plane Flight Logging System for GA & Glider

Skills used; Adobe Xd, UI Design, UX Design, System Design, Logo designing.

About the Project

Paper Plane is a flight logging app designed for groups of people who love flying, from the small group of friends who need a way to keep track of their aircraft flight records and documents to clubs and organizations such as flight schools and gliding clubs. Using a clean and simple design this system is designed to keep logging aircraft simple and easy.
