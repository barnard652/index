Hi, am Matthew

A dedicated designer based in the UK, passionate about advancing my career in design and UI/UX.

Throughout my career, I have worked on a wide range of diverse projects, from startups to international brands, creating impactful user experiences, websites, and illustrations, and shaping visual identities and other graphic assets.

My work spans print and digital mediums, focusing on digital projects. To do this I am fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, After Effects) as well as Figma, and Zeplin when working on web-based applications.

In previous roles, I created branding, UI and graphic assets like illustrations for bespoke web-based applications for the primary care, customer services, and international learning platforms.

Through these projects, I played an instrumental role in implementing up-to-date design practices and introducing new processes such as Atomic Design and in-depths design systems for both designers and developers.

In my next role, I hope to use this knowledge to continue delivering high-quality designs. However, I am eager to learn from others to expand my knowledge and remain committed to continuous learning and adding new dimensions to my work.
