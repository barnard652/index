Background

Hi, my name is Matthew Barnard, a 22-year-old multi-disciplined designer based in the UK. My experience ranges from Digital designs such as UI/UX Design, Web Design and Motion Graphics to Branding, Type design and Front-End Development with a well-recognised background in Print formats such as Book Cover and Edital design. Demonstrated by being a shortlisted for Penguin Random House student design awards 2018 and receiving a Commendation from the University of Northampton for my achievements.

After leaving university I went to work into a digital agency where I work on projects for Morrisons, Aunt Bessie, Food Doctor and Starbucks doing some print based work but focusing mainly on digital ordering systems, user interfaces and websites.

As a Designer, I believe that design can change the world and solve real-world problems. I have a passion for learning new skills, designing visual languages, solving complex problems using narrative and storytelling in my design, believing in creating meaningful and emotional reactions using graphics for the better.

My passion for design is shown by my first class Hons degree in BA Hons Graphic Communication and the drive to complete a Master Degree in the future to expand my skills and knowledge in this fascinating area.Outside of the studio, you can find me at the airfield where I fly gliders from Buckminster Gliding Club where I am building my skill as a Pilot, both flying Glider and Motor Glider.

Skills: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Dreamweaver, Adobe Xd, Adobe Animate, Adobe Aftereffects, HTML5, CSS3, Photography, BirdFont
