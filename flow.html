PROJECT:
FLOW, BRANDING FOR A NEW WAY TO SHOP.

Skills used; illustrator, Brand Design, Logo Design, Motion Graphic.

About The Project

Flow is smart, she remembers to look online to get you the best deal before you click Add to Basket. Shop like normal and we'll find every place to shop online and get you the best deal for the items you want to buy whether you are on Amazon, eBay, New Look or any offer online store. Flow takes the title and description of the item and finds the best matches so you can get what you what for the best price.
