    Skills Used: Illustrator, Xd and Figma

    About The Project

    Novel Idea is an innovative bookstore concept, emerging from the need to create a mock website for new team members to practice building websites using various platforms like WordPress and Laravel. In discovering the vast collection of book covers available at the href=“http://bookcoverarchive.com/”>Book Cover Archive, I drew inspiration from the unique and edgy designs that exuded an indie vibe. My target audience was young, 'hipster', and design-oriented - a reflection of myself. To make the mock website feel like a real client project, I developed a brand guideline that featured a wooden font, with bold sans-serif elements and a simple blue color scheme that accentuated the white text.

    The Website

    The Novel Idea website features a minimalist design that perfectly complements its branding. Bold typography and prominent book covers take center stage, providing users with an immersive browsing experience reminiscent of strolling through a physical bookstore. The design was inspired by the form of books themselves, with filters placed on the side, rotated 90 degrees to resemble book spines on a shelf. The clean grid layout beautifully showcases all the titles, making it easy for users to find what they're looking for. These design choices make for an engaging and visually appealing website that leaves a lasting impression on book lovers.
