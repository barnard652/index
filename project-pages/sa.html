About The Project

Over the past year, I have used the Apple Watch Fitness app to track my swimming and keep a log of all my past swims. I am now getting to a stage where the app is not giving me the information I need to improve my swimming, with no form of feedback in the swim session. This project aims to give the user a higher level of feedback simply and clearly, that improves the user's swimming.

One of the goals was to find a way to display swim data across multiple days, weeks, and months, to understand if the user was improving or heading downstream.
