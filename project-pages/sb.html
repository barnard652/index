About The Project

Sometimes you need someone with the skills and tools you simply don't have to do your project. It might be a broken gate, or maybe something mechanical that needs fixing, or a metal sculpture that needs a little TLC. SPARK is a domestic welding and metalwork service that comes straight to your job when you need them. From one-off jobs that need doing by a professional, to one-off works of art, Spark can help you do the job right.

The Branding Aplications

As both the company and branding are strong and robust by their nature, it meant that all applications of the branding needed to be the same; from the tools to the workplace, the brand needed to fit and stand out on all surfaces. Thanks to the strong colour combination and vitality of graphical elements relating to the branding, the addition of the logo or brand marking on any surface is clean and strong.
